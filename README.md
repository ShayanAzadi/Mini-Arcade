## ğŸ•¹ï¸ Games Included

### 1. Pong
Player 1: A / D  
Player 2: Arrow Keys  
The first player to score 10 goals wins.

### 2. Wrestling
Controls: WASD / Q and Arrow Keys / O  
Speed increases during the game.  
If a player hits a wall, the opponent gains a point.  
Press O and Q to increase power. When two players collide, the player with higher power wins the clash.  
The player with more points wins the game.

### 3. SUMO
Controls: Q & O  
Two players try to push each other.  
If a player is pushed out of the arena, the opponent gains a point.

### 4. Target Destroyer
Targets appear randomly.  
Click them to earn points.  
If a target disappears before being clicked, you lose points.  
If your score reaches 0, you lose the game.

### 5. Memory Cards
Match identical cards to earn points.  
The player with more points at the end wins.

### 6. Snake
Classic snake game.  
Avoid hitting yourself and try to eat the apples.

### 7. XO
Two-player turn-based game with atom and anti-atom themes.  
You can choose different skins in the main menu.  
The first player to connect three marks in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins.  
If the game ends in a draw, a bomb randomly appears on one square. The player can click a square to change its icon.

### 8. Little Powerful Bird
Control the bird using the Space key and avoid obstacles.  
Collision ends the game.

### 9. Race
Two players control cars using the O and Q keys.  
The first player to reach the finish line wins.

### 10. Broken Cars
Controls: Q / O  
One player attacks while the other defends.  
If the cars collide, the attacker gains a point.  
If the defender dodges, roles switch and the defender gains a point.  
The first player to reach 10 points wins.
